About Us

ISD (Isibusiso Sama Dlamini) Hygiene and Trolley Services (PTY) LTD is a 100% black youth-owned company established in 2024 to fulfill the needs of contract cleaning and trolley management services in South Africa. Registered in 2025 with company registration number 2025/146864/07, we pride ourselves on our vast experience in the cleaning and hygiene industry.

The owner and director, Mr. Nqubeko Mlondi Dlamini, brings over four years of hands-on and managerial experience in this field. Our history dates back to 2019, when the founder identified a gap in the market to apply his skills, focusing on skills transfer and community benefit rather than profit alone.

Our Services

Cleaning Division

We offer contract cleaning services for industries such as commercial, construction, office spaces, resorts, retail, and hotels. Our specialized services include:

  • Office Cleaning Services
  • Buildings Cleaning Services
  • Shopping Malls Cleaning Services
  • Specialized Domestic Cleaning Services
  • Parks Maintenance and Garden Services

Trolley Division

We specialize in trolley management services, ensuring they are hygienic and in good use for customers.

Mission Statement

ISD Hygiene and Trolley Services (PTY) LTD has risen from a disadvantaged background to become a well-recognized business in a short period. We are dedicated to delivering outstanding service to our clients, including offices, malls, and shopping centers, with loyalty and trustworthiness. Our pricing is competitive, and we strive to leave a positive reputation wherever we work.

Business Strategy

Currently based in the KwaZulu-Natal (KZN) region, our goal is to expand our services throughout South Africa, including cities like Johannesburg and Cape Town. We focus on hiring and training well-qualified individuals to maintain our high standards. Our strategy prioritizes respecting our clients, listening to their needs, and delivering exceptional service without fail.

Our target market includes businesses, municipalities, private and public companies, and private households.
