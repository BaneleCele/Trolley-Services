About Us

ISD (Isibusiso Sama Dlamini) Hygiene and Trolley Division is a company established in 2024 to fulfill the needs of contract cleaning and trolley management services in South Africa.

Our Services

Cleaning Division

We offer contract cleaning services for industries such as commercial, construction, office spaces, resorts, retail, and hotels.

Trolley Division

We specialize in trolley management services, ensuring they are hygienic and in good use for customers.
